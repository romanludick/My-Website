Hi, I'm Roman Ludick

My journey began in the heart of **Botswana**, where I grew up in a small but vibrant town filled with love, football, and big dreams.

Growing Up in Botswana

As a kid, football was my escape. We played barefoot on dusty fields, using makeshift goals made of rocks. Every evening, my friends and I would gather under the African sunset, dreaming of playing for the biggest clubs in the world.

My parents always supported my love for the game, but they also emphasized the importance of education. I balanced school with my passion for football, training every chance I got. Little did I know, my talent would take me beyond Botswana’s borders.

The Call from Spain

One day, my life changed forever. A scout from FC Barcelona had come to Botswana to observe young talents. I gave it everything I had during the trials, and before I knew it, I was invited to train at **La Masia**, Barcelona's legendary academy.

Moving to Spain at a young age was both exciting and terrifying. I had to learn a new language, adapt to a different culture, and compete with some of the best young players in the world. But every struggle made me stronger. I trained alongside future legends like **Lionel Messi and Neymar**, pushing myself to the limit every day.

Lessons from Football

Football taught me more than just how to play the game—it taught me discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Training at La Masia shaped me into the person I am today, both on and off the field.

Life Beyond Football: Family Comes First

Eventually, my journey took a different turn. While football had been my life, I found something even greater—**love and family**. Today, I’m happily married and the proud father of beautiful kids.

Now, I find joy in teaching my children the same lessons I learned on the field—**hard work, passion, and never giving up on their dreams**. Football will always be a part of me, but my greatest victory is the life I’ve built with my family.
